Stopping Sugar

I really like sugary foods. My single sitting sugary feats include twelve (12) cream-filled or iced donuts (3000+ calories), 20 chocolate chip cookies (3600 calories), and a few more that I'd rather not admit in a public forum. Is it a problem with self-control? Possibly...

Regardless, if I could never crave sugary food again, I'd be happy: it's healthy and I can get food satisfaction elsewhere. Here I abstain from self-proclaimed sugary foods for 31 days (01 January 2021 - 31 January 2021) and subjectively measure the intensity of sugar cravings.
