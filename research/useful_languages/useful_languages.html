Useful Languages

Introduction and Scope

This essay is meant to summarize the usefulness of six different foreign languages for native English speakers. The languages are:

  • Arabic
  • Chinese Mandarin
  • French
  • German
  • Russian
  • Spanish

Other languages will not be considered, as the number of speakers is relatively low, the job opportunities in those countries are low, and only a few countries/regions speak the language.

Usefulness will be quantified and qualified by the following. Note that all of the above will be independent of personal reasons, such as heritage, cultural interest, etc. It will be based strictly on usefulness of what the language can get you monetarily, travel-wise, and connection-wise.

Speakers

The total number of both current and future speakers will be counted/estimated. Sources differ on the total number, so multiple will be taken into account.

The future number of speakers will be of the year 2030 (10 years from when this essay was written).

Geographic Spread

How widely is the language spoken throughout the world? Mandarin is spoken throughout a large majority of China, but is also spoken in Chinatowns throughout the world.

Languages that are spoken less frequently in more countries will be considered better than those that are spoken more frequently in fewer countries.

Difficulty of Acquisition for English Speakers

How difficult is it for a native English speaker to reach conversational levels of the target language? The more difficult, the less useful.
