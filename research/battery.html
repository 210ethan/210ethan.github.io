I was (am?) notorious amongst my friends and family for having poor battery life. One friend and I had a running joke that whenever I met up with him, my phone was at less than 20% battery. While it may be availability bias, it probably happened more often than not.

But is this truly my fault (probably)? Do I use my phone too much (probably)? Or is my low phone battery a product of certain conditions that drain my battery faster than normal? In an attempt to exonerate myself, I ran the following experiments:

  • Baseline battery test
  • Volume
  • Brightness
  • Open apps in the background
  • Flashlight

Experiments

All experiments were done with the following (taken from the Settings/General/About screen):

  • Software version: 15.6.1
  • Model Name: iPhone SE
  • Model Number: A2275

The phone was charged to 100%, left plugged in for five minutes more, then unplugged and the experiment started.
