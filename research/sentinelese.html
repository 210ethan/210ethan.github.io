Background

The Sentinelese inhabit North Sentinel Island in the Indian Ocean. Despite having been contacted by a handful of outsiders, they are effectively isolated from the modern world, guarded by the Indian navy and a hefty distrust of non-Sentinelese people. A majority of individuals who have landed on the island have been met with hostility in the form of bows and arrows.

This prompts the obvious question: how would one go about trying to exterminate every last Sentinelese on the island?
