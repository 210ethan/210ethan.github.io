Moderation is the Enemy and I Will Wage War on It Wherever I Go

No, not everything is okay in moderation.

I've accepted I have a problem with self-control, at least in some aspects of my life. It's something I noticed as a kid, but as an adult it's a much riskier characteristic to have, and as a result, I have declared an all-out war on moderation.

I first heard "everything is fine in moderation" from a family member when I was young. I ate that garbag up and ran with it.
