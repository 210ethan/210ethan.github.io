Background

People travel a lot. There were 969 million tourists in 2022 with even more expected in 2023. Massive industries exist to support travelers' comfortability. Workplace discussions often include talk of someone's recent or upcoming trip. People flex their souvenirs and pictures in their homes or on their person.

But what is the purpose of traveling?
