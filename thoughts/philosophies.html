List

  • Feedback is almost always valuable and worth seeking out. Find a reasonable, friendly person who can provide honest feedback. See one-on-ones.
  • Output scales superlinearly with effort. Deliberate efforts produce both more results and higher quality results.
  • Financial status contributes significantly to overall well-being.
  • Luck plays a much bigger role than people understand and a much, much bigger role than people give it credit for.
  • Expect any secret told to another another to get out at some point.
  • Any future expected free time will be unexpectedly taken.
