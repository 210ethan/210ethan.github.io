Home Gym: Optimizing for Space and Cost

What should the requirements be for a home gym?

  1. Minimal footprint: The gym should not take up more space than is necessary.
  2. Minimal equipment: The amount of equipment is generally proportional to the footprint, so this should also be minimized. The equipment should also be the most bang-for-your-buck.

With those requirements in mind, what equipment should you get? The answer is simple:

  • Squat rack
  • Pull-up bar
  • Bench
  • Olympic barbell + 300-400 lb plates
  • Optional:
    • Gymnastic rings
    • Dumbbells (fixed or adjustable)
    • Attachable dip bars
