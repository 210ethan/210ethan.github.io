Prerequisites

The OAEL requires basic strength and balance skills, which can be satisified by the following prerequisites:

  • Two-arm elbow lever: This should be an effortless hold for both strength and balance.
  • 20 pushups: Basic pushing strength.
  • Handstand (recommended): OAEL uses the wrists and cambered hand technique to adjust balance. This is also used in HS.
  • Wrist strength: All of your bodyweight is going onto one hand/wrist, so it needs to be strong enough to support that.

Progressions

Weight Shift

While in normal EL, slowly shift weight to one side. Eventually no weight will be resting on the other arm and it will be free to lift.

Training Plan

With skills involving balance, practicing as frequently as possible and with the best form as possible is key. Progress will be significantly greater if you practice 5 day/week for 30 min than it is 1 day/week for 150 min.
