Everesting

The Rules

The official rules are listed here, but can be boiled down to a few basic bullet points:

  • Total elevation gain must be at least 29,032 ft (8,849 m).
  • Everesting must be completed in one activity.
  • Everesting must be completed on a single hill, i.e., you must go up and down the same hill CEILING(29,032/[elevation gain in feet]) times.

A few extra rules I am imposing on myself:

  • No clothing changes. This includes shoes, socks, shorts, and shirts.
