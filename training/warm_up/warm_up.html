Purpose

Crossfit provides a good overview on the purpose of a warm-up in their A Better Warm-up:

Increase body temperature and heart rate, provide some stretching, stimulate the entire body and major biomechanical functions, provide practice for basic movements, and finally, prepare for rigorous athletic training
