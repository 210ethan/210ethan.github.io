Favorite and Important Books

Used vs. New Books

Unless you are a huge fan of the series or author, new books are pointless. They cost more without giving more information and you can likely find a used book that is in pristine, almost-new condition for a fraction of the price.

I currently use BookFinder.com to find any book I'm wanting to purchase. Simply enter the author, title, or ISBN and the site searches every major bookseller online to find you the cheapest option. Free, fast, and simple to use.
