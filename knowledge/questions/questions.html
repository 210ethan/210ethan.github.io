  • What makes some materials more flammable than others? From chemistry.stackexchange: "for a material to be flammable it needs to contain something that is oxidisable by air oxygen, typically carbon ... The more volatile a material is, the more flammable it will be." Volatility is determined by the material's intermolecular forces.
